About me

I am Luis, originally from the second region of Antofagasta, Chile. I embarked on my academic journey by enrolling in a Bachelor of Arts with a focus on Musical Composition at the Universidad de Chile in 2014. Concurrently, I pursued a degree in Computer Engineering, developing a profound interest in electroacoustic music and Machine Learning. Upon completing both degrees, I decided to study for a Bachelor of Performance in Piano. I am passionate about teaching the subjects I learn. I have worked both as an engineer and as a composer, contributing to small audiovisual projects. My strengths lie in research and experimentation. In the realm of academic music, my major influences are Morton Feldman and Iannis Xenakis.
